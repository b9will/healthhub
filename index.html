The Problem

Health & wellness is broken into a hundred disconnected pieces.

Patients juggle multiple portals, scattered records, and endless phone calls to manage their health. Practitioners lose clients between visits and have no scalable way to share their expertise. The system fails both sides.

üîç

Discovery is Fragmented

Finding the right practitioner means sifting through outdated directories, unreliable reviews, and endless Google searches with no way to filter by what actually matters.

üìã

Records Live Everywhere

Every practitioner has their own portal, their own login, their own system. Patients become the middleman between their own care providers, repeating information at every visit.

üí∞

Practitioners Hit a Ceiling

Income is capped at the number of hours in a day. There's no scalable infrastructure for practitioners to monetize their expertise through courses, content, or digital products.

The Solution

One platform. Three powerful pillars.

Good Health Hub connects every piece of the wellness journey into a single, elegant experience for patients and practitioners alike.

01

Discover & Book

A curated, searchable directory of vetted wellness practitioners. Filter by specialty, location, availability, insurance, and treatment philosophy. Book directly with zero friction.

02

Your Health Home

A persistent patient dashboard that centralizes appointment history, practitioner notes, saved providers, and recommended resources. Your health story, all in one place.

03

Learn & Grow

A knowledge marketplace where practitioners sell courses, host live webinars, and publish premium content. Patients get expert-led education; practitioners unlock scalable revenue.

Business Model

Three revenue streams. One flywheel.

Every side of the platform reinforces the other, creating compounding network effects.

Patients

Freemium Access

Free for patients to search, book, and manage appointments. Premium tier unlocks advanced health tracking, priority booking, and exclusive content.

Free / $9.99 premium

Pro Subscriptions
